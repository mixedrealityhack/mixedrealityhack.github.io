RMIT'S FIRST MIXED REALITY HACKATHON

2 days, 20 teams, endless possibilities. Aim big with mixed reality technologies.

WHAT TO EXPECT

Free food, fun and learning. Intensive collaboration over 2 days, with workshops designed to get you hands on with Windows Mixed Reality and the HoloLens so you can design, create and deploy your solution.

MORE THAN CODE

Everyone’s invited - and everyone’s needed! A strong team consists of Designers, Hustlers and Hackers. People with Psychology, Education, Marketing and Business experience are encouraged to apply!

NETWORKING & MENTORS

Reinventing the way we learn goes beyond this hackathon. Collaborate and learn from people who live and breathe Mixed Reality. Microsoft and RMIT mentors with hands-on experience are here to help you!

YOUR CHALLENGE

In today’s Learn-it-all society, the way we learn isn’t keeping up. Your aim is to create a solution using new emerging technologies like Mixed Reality, Machine Learning, AI and Spatial computing to come up with new ways to learn.

NO EXPERIENCE? NO PROBLEM!

We’re here to empower people of all experience levels to aim big by providing you the resources, support and tools you’ll need to make your dreams a reality. We’ve got food and workshops covered, it’s up to you to bring your creative problem solving skills!

HOW DO I CHOOSE THE RIGHT TICKET?

Hackers

For the programmers and those comfortable with code. You're integral to helping integrate Azure services and deploying the solution.

Designers

For the visionaries, graphic and interface designers, and those who breathe UX. You'll help make it look and feel good for users.

Hustlers

For those who have a strong psychology, marketing or business background. You're key to bringing each piece of the project together!

Team

You can register as a team. Don't worry if you don't have a team or haven't registered it when you get your ticket, we'll be in contact to sort out all the details.
